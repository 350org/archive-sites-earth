Fossil of the Day

350.org has worked with partners CANinternational and Avaaz to present the “Fossil of the Day” awards at UN Climate Conferences in Cancun, Mexico (COP17) and Lima, Peru (COP20). The Fossil of the Day is an awarded given each day during the COPs to the country who is the best at being the worst, and who has done the most to prevent the UNFCCC from reaching a fair, just and binding climate agreement. This humorous award ceremony is an important tool used to call out the countries and corporations that are actively presenting progress on international climate legislation.
